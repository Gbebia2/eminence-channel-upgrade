Our Vision & Leadership

Meet Evangelist Celyna Ebohon: Founder & Minister

Eminence Channel Ministries is a dedicated platform providing resources for spiritual growth and a closer walk with God. Our Foundation: Mission, Vision, and Values

The guiding principles that drive our ministry and our commitment to serving God and His people.

Our Core Mission

Provide a broad range of religious, social, and charitable services to individuals, families, and missionaries, driven by a deep commitment to service and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

To see every believer spiritually readjusted, reinstated, replenished, and renewed, transforming their communities through radical faith and deeper intimacy in the secret place.

Our Core Values

  • Passionate trust in God and the power of prayer.
  • Serving people with compassion, conveying mercy and love.
  • Valuing spiritual, emotional, and social growth equally.

Full Bio & Credentials

Evangelist Celyna Ebohon is the founder of Eminence Channel Ministries. A ministry on a mission to provide a broad range of religious and social services to individuals, families, and missionaries.

She is an intercessor, a counselor, and a speaker. She assists people to overcome trauma, pain point, and things that are holding them back. She is passionate about spending time with God in the secret place and currently coordinates a team of intercessors who meet twice a week on the prayer line where lives are touched through motivation, inspiration, and trusting God.

She holds a Master’s degree in Administration (MBA) and currently a doctoral candidate in Christian counselling with emphasis on marriage, family, and child therapy. Featured in Professional Organization of Women of Excellence Recognized (P.O.W.E.R.) as VIP in Spring 2023 magazine. She is on social media platforms including YouTube.
